Currentium — for every tool your USB power bank can’t handle.

Currentium is a 12V-native modular power bank concept that combines the portability of USB power banks with the usefulness of a larger portable power station.

Core prototype claims

  • 75Wh / 24,000mAh capacity
  • 120W max output / 10A
  • 12.8V native output
  • 730g module weight
  • Solar, car, boat and wall charging
  • LFP chemistry with 2000+ cycle class
  • Scalable modular design for field work, film, emergency preparedness, medical work, search and rescue, nature conservation and solar cooking
